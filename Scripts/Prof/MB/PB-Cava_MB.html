Welcome to Mining + Blacksmithing Professional Buddy profile



What this profile does:

This profile will raise your Mining and Blacksmithing professions from level any level to 600.

The bot will farm all needed materials by mining or grinding.

While farming, the bot will switch maps to avoid detection

The bot will use materials in the personal bank to speed up the leveling progress.

You can select what bot will do crafted materials as well as excess farmed materials(selling at vendor/AH , dropping in Guild Bank or Mailling to an alt).

The bot will farm or grind, raise professions levels and swich continents without human interaction. This profile is 100% AFK.




What this profile needs:

A level 90 character

Character must know how to travel to and from (Know Portals):

-Mount Hyjal

-Twilight Highlands

-Deepholm

-Pandarian

Character must know how to fly in all the continents

Have as much bag and bank space as possible. This will speed up the plugin and avoid unnecessary runs to deposit materialss in the bank.

Time, raising mining and blacksmithing from level 1 to 600 need some time, isnt easy or fast farm all ores, smelt ores, craft gear/weapons, etc.




Extras:

Clean your bags before starting this profile, leave only Hearth Stone, Mining/Blacksmithing Tools and Food/Water. The bot will sell, mail or deposit everything else in the Guild Bank.

When you stop the profile, the bot will continue where it left off

If you start the bot, or the bot is in a main city (and not grinding/farming), it will do checks to ensure all the items and materials are in the correct places.

Theres an option to alow bot use the Hearth Stone. This will speed up the process of going back to the main cities (bot will save at the closest main city, if enable).

The first time the profile is started, you will see this text and bot will wait untill youre ready. The bot will then do some tests and check to see if the character knows all the needed portals.
